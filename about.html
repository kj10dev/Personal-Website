My Life Story

Ive been facinated by technology and computers since I was a small child. I didnt come from a rich family but we were fortunate enough to have a computer which I started playing on at age 9 already. We had windows XP, I remember experimenting alot with the settings and customizing whatever I could but I was forture enough to never compromize the operting system.

By age 13 I had already dismantled and reassembled the hardware components without my parents knowing. I gained alot of exposure to the internet from a young age but I regret not informing myself about web development or penetration testing in those days.

In high school I enrolled myself in IT and started programming in Delphi at 15. After high school I struggled to get into university for Computer Science, which made me believe this might not be my path. I also have a big passion for business and finance so I decided to pursue that career. Things started working out, life became prosperous until 2017. My family started struggling financially so I was forced to drop out of university.

2018 was tough, I could neither study nor find employment. The big positve was I discovered blockchain technology which rekindled my passion for technology with blend of finance. The following year I would enroll in an distant learning university for Computer Science which is what I had always wanted, the only catch was I needed to teach myself everything. This was a challenge initially but I realised a fair share of the industry is self-taught and once a concept is grasped it yours forever.

I am currently completing my degree and informing myself about various technology to stay updated and to speed track myself to my first developer job. I aspire to become a Full-stack & Mobile App developer.
